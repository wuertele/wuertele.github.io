About Espalight

We're geeks about lighting, open-source enthusiasts, and passionate about rethinking what's possible with home and office lighting control. Espalight gives control and choice back to you.

  • Transparency: Entirely open-source from hardware to software.
  • Flexibility: Customize beyond closed systems like Lutron.
  • Innovation: A fresh approach to lighting quality and reliability.

Key Advantages

  • Open Source: Endless customizability, no vendor lock-in.
  • ESP32 & ESPHome Integration: Seamless Home Assistant compatibility.
  • No PWM—Superior Lighting Quality: Flicker-free, interference-free lighting.
  • PoE-Powered Simplicity: One cable for power and data.
  • Cost-Effective: Significant savings compared to proprietary solutions.

Ideal Use Cases

  • New Construction and Smart Renovations: Simplified installation for large residential projects.
  • Professional Installations and Integrations: For installers who value flexibility and openness.
  • Home Automation Enthusiasts: DIY-friendly for total control and customization.
